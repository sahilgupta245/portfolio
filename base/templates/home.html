About Me

Sahil Gupta

My name is Sahil Gupta, and I am currently based in Delhi. I've always been interested in technology. That's why I have completed my Master's in Computer Applications (MCA) from Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology, following a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) from Singhania University. Throughout my academic journey, I have consistently demonstrated a deep passion for technology, particularly in Python and Java development, which are my primary areas of expertise.

In addition to my formal education, I have 2 years of hands-on experience working as a Software Trainer and Data Entry Executive at Gagan Pathology Centre, where I honed my non-technical and leadership skills. During my tenure, I trained junior employees and significantly improved operational efficiency.

My enthusiasm for technology extends beyond the classroom and workplace. I have actively participated in hackathons during my Master's program, where I tackled real-world problems and collaborated with like-minded individuals to innovate and create impactful solutions.

My skill set includes proficiency in Java, Python, C/C++, and MySQL, and experience with frameworks such as Spring and Bootstrap. My recent projects, are ImagiXpert, where I developed tools using Python and OpenCV, and NexGen HR, a Spring MVC-based Employee management system, I built a register and login system and CRUD operation.
